About SLFA

Sierra Leone Football Association

Established in 1967, the Sierra Leone Football Association has been the governing body for football in Sierra Leone, dedicated to developing and promoting the beautiful game across our nation.

OUR STORY

Our Story

The Sierra Leone Football Association has been at the heart of football development in Sierra Leone for over five decades. From humble beginnings to international recognition, we have fostered talent and promoted the beautiful game across our nation, creating opportunities for players, coaches, and fans alike.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission

To develop, promote, and manage football at all levels in Sierra Leone. We are committed to creating pathways for talent development, inspiring the next generation of football stars, and representing Sierra Leone with pride on the international stage.

GENERAL INFO

General Info

Founded to govern and develop football in Sierra Leone, SLFA works tirelessly to create opportunities for players, coaches, and fans throughout the country. We oversee domestic competitions, national team selections, and youth development programs.

YOUTH DEVELOPMENT

Youth Development

Our youth development programs are the foundation of Sierra Leone football. We invest in grassroots initiatives, coaching education, and talent identification to ensure a bright future for football in our country.

OFFICIAL STORE

Official Store

Get your official SLFA merchandise, jerseys, and supporter gear. Show your pride and support for Sierra Leone football with authentic products that represent our national team and football heritage.

PLAYER SERVICES

Player Services

Professional support and career guidance for Sierra Leone's football talent. We provide assistance with international transfers, contract negotiations, and connecting players with opportunities worldwide.
